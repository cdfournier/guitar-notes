What is this thing?

I like to play guitar, but I'm not a good guitar player. So over the years I've developed my own style to compensate, and I also transcribe songs to my style. Sometimes that means changing the chords or the strumming patterns and sometimes even the lyrics. I used to know only a handful of songs that were easy enough to remember, but I continue to add more songs so it's become necessary to create a means for me to pull up my notes any time or place. I also like to design and code so I built this thing.
